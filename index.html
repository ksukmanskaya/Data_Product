Introduction

  • RShiny COVID-19 Analytics App built based on data collected by Jonh Hopkins University. For more information proceed here

  • The purpose of the App is to keep track of the pandemic and get insights on current trends

Advantages

  • Easy to use

  • Up-to-date: JHU updates their reports daily

  • Interpretable: reports on ‘Metrics’ tab include valuable information for geo region comparison

  • Valuable insights: model fitting helps to understand whats the situation in a particular region and has reached a peak yet or not

Insights: New York vs California

New cases curve (as of 2020-05-30) in New York and Califormia states. As we can see, CA doesnt seem to have reached its peak, on the contrary, NY is doing great.

Prediction example: California

Model tries to find the peak date and fit 2 separate models before and after the peak, otherwise - just 1 fit for the exponential growth.
